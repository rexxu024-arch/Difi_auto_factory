# Etsy Digital Product Blueprints

Generated: 2026-05-05 02:04 PM EDT

## Launch First: Printable Wall Art Sets

Use existing high-resolution Poster production images.

Offer:
- 5 JPG files per listing: 2x3, 3x4, 4x5, 11x14, ISO A-series.
- Instant download only.
- Personal-use license.
- AI-assisted disclosure in description.

Why this comes first:
- No customer photo handling.
- No Printify production cost.
- No shipping delay or return ambiguity.
- Very low bandwidth after the pack is generated locally.
- Fits Quiet Relic Studio's premium Zen / Dark Academia brand shell.

Suggested pricing:
- Single printable wall art listing: $8.99 or $9.99.
- 3-piece bundle: $14.99-$19.99.
- Larger themed collection: $24.99-$34.99 once visual coherence is proven.

Listing description note:
This is an instant digital download. No physical product will be shipped. Files are prepared for personal printing and home decor use.

## Second Pilot: Personalized Ex Libris / Bookplate

Use existing Academia / library / relic DNA.

Offer:
- Buyer provides name or short inscription.
- Deliver JPG/PDF bookplate in 1-2 sizes.
- One light revision included.

Why this is strong:
- More differentiated than generic printable wall art.
- Fits book lovers, dark academia, journal, library, study-room buyers.
- Personalization is text-only, so it avoids pet/family likeness risk.

Suggested pricing:
- $9.99-$14.99 basic.
- $18.99 premium with 2 variants.

Required before launch:
- Simple intake fields.
- Revision policy.
- Template script for text placement.

## Third Pilot: Wallpaper Packs

Use Acrylic and Poster art crops.

Offer:
- Phone wallpaper.
- Desktop wallpaper.
- Tablet wallpaper.
- Square social/avatar crop.

Why this is useful:
- It gives lower-price products to a shop that otherwise sells premium physical items.
- It can reuse vertical Acrylic images that are lower resolution than Poster art but still suitable for screens.

Suggested pricing:
- $3.99-$5.99.

Concern:
- Lower buyer intent than printable decor, so keep as add-on rather than main shop identity.

## Later: Custom Pet Familiar Portrait

Use only after the revision/QA system exists.

Offer:
- Buyer uploads pet photo.
- Choose one style: Dark Academia Familiar, Zen Guardian, Celestial Companion.
- Deliver one digital portrait plus one phone wallpaper crop.

Suggested pricing:

## Defer: Digital Family / Couple Portrait

Not recommended for the current automation stage.

Reason:
- Higher emotional stakes and more revisions.
- Likeness expectations are much harder than object/artwork products.
- This can be built later after pet portrait workflow is stable.

## Default Launch Sequence

1. Build 20 printable wall art listings from best Poster images.
2. Upload them to Etsy after shop shell is finalized.
3. Watch impressions, favorites, and clicks for 3-7 days.
4. Create 10 personalized bookplate templates if printable art shows traffic.
5. Prototype 5 pet familiar portraits only after intake/revision workflow is ready.
