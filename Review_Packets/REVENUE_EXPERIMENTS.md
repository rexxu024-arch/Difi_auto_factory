# OpenClaw Revenue Experiments

Updated: 2026-05-06 New York time

## Current Operating View

The core business goal is not simply "make more listings." The goal is to find repeatable product-market fit, then turn the winning DNA into a mostly automated production and feedback loop.

Current constraints:

- Etsy login is temporarily blocked, so Etsy UI edits and publish actions are paused.
- Printify/eBay production works.
- Wired network is stable, so online batches are allowed again.
- eBay traffic is still weak, so ads alone should not be treated as the answer.

## Best Near-Term Income Experiments

### 1. Etsy Digital Printable Wall Art Packs

Why this is first:

- Uses existing Poster/Acrylic art assets.
- No Printify production cost, no shipping risk, no production mismatch.
- Etsy official fees are low: $0.20/listing and a 4-month listing period.
- Etsy allows original digital designs, and AI-assisted work must be disclosed in the listing.

Execution:

- Convert strong Poster designs into multi-size printable packs.
- Suggested pack contents: 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, 5:7, 11x14 ratio JPEG/PDF files.
- Initial price test: $4.99, $6.99, $8.99.
- Start with 10-20 packs after Etsy login recovers.

Success metric:

- Favorites/views in 72 hours.
- First sale or add-to-cart within 2 weeks.
- If no traffic, test broader SEO phrases before adding volume.

Sources:

- Etsy fee policy: https://www.etsy.com/legal/fees/
- Etsy what can be sold / digital and AI disclosure: https://help.etsy.com/hc/en-us/articles/360024112614-What-Can-I-Sell-on-Etsy

### 2. eBay Traffic Recovery A/B Cells

Why this matters:

- Current eBay listings have attractive art but poor traffic.
- Fixed 2% General ads did not by itself solve zero-view state.
- eBay needs controlled experiments, not just more POD listings.

Execution cells:

- Control: leave a small group unchanged.
- Title-intent rewrite: buyer terms first, aesthetic terms second.
- Price test: lower selected Sticker sets or add offer psychology.
- Cover/trust test: strongest official mockup first, profile/shop trust fixed.
- Product-mix test: Poster/Acrylic rather than more Sticker.

Success metric:

- 24h/72h views.
- Impressions once eBay API/Seller Hub reporting is stable.
- Favorites/watchers/click-through.

Decision:

- If multiple tested cells remain near-zero, eBay becomes a secondary channel and Etsy becomes primary.

### 3. Curated Premium Etsy POD Launch

Why:

- Etsy audience is more aligned with decor, wall art, gifts, and aesthetic products.
- Current Poster/Acrylic are visually stronger than Sticker as premium goods.

Execution:

- Do not dump 100 items.
- Launch 20-30 curated listings first.
- Use poetic but searchable titles and 13 tags.
- Brand shell first: Quiet Relic Studio or equivalent premium shop identity.

Blocked by:

- Etsy login/account issue.
- Etsy API pending/inactive.
- Printify Etsy mockup path needs one more QA pass.

### 4. Custom Portrait / Personalized Digital Product Pilot

Why:

- Higher willingness to pay than generic art.
- Can use the same QA pipeline with human review.

Best fit:

- Pet memorial portrait.
- Dark academia pet portrait.
- Zen ink pet portrait.
- Couple/family photo into gallery-style wall art.

Risks:

- Buyer messaging, revisions, and refunds.
- Needs intake form and clear delivery timeline.
- Should not be automated fully until QA is very stable.

Initial test:

- 3 private draft templates only, no broad launch.
- $19-$39 digital-only.

### 5. Adobe Stock / Stock Asset Pipeline

Why:

- Potential passive library income.
- Can reuse art, textures, patterns, and isolated design elements.

Why not first:

- Slower validation.
- Review/compliance heavier.
- Need metadata and AI disclosure discipline.

Source:

- Adobe Stock generative AI guidance: https://helpx.adobe.com/stock/contributor/help/generative-ai-content.html

### 6. B2B Etsy Seller Brand Packs

Why:

- We already built Etsy banner/icon/brand-shell logic.
- Other small sellers need shop branding, banners, icons, and listing mockup templates.

Execution:

- Sell digital template packs: shop icon, banner, thank-you card, listing frame.
- Use a few aesthetic niches: botanical, dark academia, spa/zen, handmade craft.

Risks:

- Needs Canva/editable templates or very clear file deliverables.
- Market may be crowded.
